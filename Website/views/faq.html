Q. Which languages can I submit my bot in?
Ans: You can submit your bot in any language you like. In case we are not able to get its interpreter/compiler, you should be able to point us to an interpreter for your program. The current starter pack supports only C/C++. Feel free to download the source code and make the necessary tweaks for your langauge. Otherwise, kindly wait till the next release to be updated soon.

Q. Will my bot work when the engine is updated?
Ans: Yes. We will take care to make sure all engines are backward compatible.

Q. Will my ranking matter if I submit earlier or later?
Ans: No, you always play enough games till your ranking stabilizes.

Q. I think there is a bug in the engine / viewer?
Ans: Please do drop us a mail with your viewer file and the bot that you used when you stumbled across the bug. We'll rush to fix it. If you've already fixed the bug, send in a patch and we'll review and apply it right away.
